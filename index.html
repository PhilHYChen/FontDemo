Noto Sans TC

簡介

Noto is a global font collection for writing in all modern and ancient languages. Noto Sans TC is an unmodulated ("sans serif") design for languages in Taiwan and Macau that use the Traditional Chinese variant of the Han ideograms. It also supports Hiragana, Katakana, Latin, Cyrillic, Greek and Hangul. It has multiple weights. —Google Fonts

樣式

Noto Sans TC comes in 6 different font weights: Thin, Light, Regular, Medium, Bold and Black.

Source Han Sans has five versions: Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese for Taiwan, Traditional Chinese for Hong Kong, Japanese and Korean. Because of the different conventions and standards in each region, the same character in Unicode may have different shapes in different versions. ——Wikipedia

其他選項

思源黑體是一套開源字型，允許任何人在遵循其授權條款的情況下逕行改作，也因此有了眾多的衍生字型。例如：

  • 源樣黑體
  • 源石黑體
  • 源泉圓體
  • 台北黑體
  • 未來熒黑
